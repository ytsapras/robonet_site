Data release policy

Our team uses the LCO global network of robotic telescopes to perform observations of microlensing events in the Galactic Bulge with the aim of discovering new extrasolar planets.

Continuous automated assessment of the observations and detection of anomalous features is provided by the ARTEMiS system. Please note that the data available on these pages are based on preliminary reductions. These reductions are updated often, as and when new data become available. If you would like to use our data for publications, please contact us (ytsapras[at]ari.uni-heidelberg.de). We appreciate the opportunity to collaborate and may require co-authorship where appropriate.
